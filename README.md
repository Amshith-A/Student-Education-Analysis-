# Student-Education-Analysis-
A comprehensive dataset analyzing student academic performance, attendance, lifestyle factors, and extracurricular participation, including pre-aggregated KPIs and data cuts.
##Dataset Used
- <a href="https://github.com/Amshith-A/Student-Education-Analysis-/blob/main/Education_Dataset.xlsx">DataSet</a>

## 📊 Dataset Description

The core dataset (Education_Dataset.csv) contains detailed records of students, encompassing demographic, academic, and lifestyle variables.

## Key Features (Columns):


Student_ID: Unique identifier for each student.

Age & Gender: Demographic information.

Course & Year_of_Study: Academic profile (e.g., BSc CS, MBA, BTech).

CGPA: Cumulative Grade Point Average.

Exam_Frequency: Number of exams taken.

Attendance_%: Percentage of classes attended.

Living_Type: Accommodation status (Day Scholar, Hostel, Room).

Club_Participation: Extracurricular involvement (Tech, Sports, Cultural, Literature, None).

Social_Media_Usage_hrs: Daily hours spent on social media.

Career_Anxiety_Level: Self-reported level of career-related stress (Scale 1-10).

Sleep_Hours: Average nightly sleep duration.

Counseling_Access: Whether the student has access to counseling services (Yes/No).

## 🔍 Key Analyses & Insights Performed
Based on the aggregated data and pivot tables, the following key analyses were conducted:

Top Scorers Analysis (Top scorer by gender from years.csv)

Identification of the highest-achieving students across different academic years and genders, with top scores reaching up to 9.99 and 10.0 CGPA.

Attendance Trends (Attandance by year.csv)

Tracking how student attendance varies across the 1st, 2nd, 3rd, and 4th years of study. (e.g., highest cumulative attendance is typically observed in the 1st and 2nd years).

Academic Performance by Field (cgpa by field of study by year.csv)

Evaluating the average CGPA across different courses (BSc CS, BBA, BTech, BCA, MCA) and how it fluctuates year over year. Average CGPAs generally hover around 6.9 to 7.1.

Impact of Extracurriculars (club participation by attandanc.csv)

Analyzing the correlation between club participation (Tech, Sports, Literature, Cultural) and average attendance. It explores whether active students maintain better attendance and CGPA.

Lifestyle & Accommodation (living type by sleep.csv)

Investigating the relationship between a student's living arrangement (Hostel, Day Scholar, Rented Room) and their average sleep hours.

Overall KPIs (kpis & slicers.csv)

Average CGPA: ~6.98

Average Attendance: ~74.59%

Average Sleep Hours: ~6.76 hours

Average Social Media Usage: ~4.51 hours

Average Career Anxiety Level: ~5.45 / 10
